Why own a dog?

Improved Health

Studies have shown that pet ownership can enhance fitness levels, lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels, and improve your heart health.

Better Habits

A research study found that dog owners more likely report regular physical activity patterns, a healthy diet and ideal blood sugar levels compared to those who do not own dogs.

Mental Health

Owning a dog has been linked to better mental health and less perception of social isolation — both risk factors for heart attacks.

Get Social

Dog owners have increased interaction with people, and are less likely to experience depression.
